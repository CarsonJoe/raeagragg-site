How did a college kid build something like MUP? The project started at Loyola Marymount University School of Film and Television, then expanded into a full narrative world and cast.

Development

How Mup was Born

“I asked myself, what if I made a story that a kid like me would have actually read?” That question shaped the tone: wild, adventurous, and emotionally real. Early notes also framed Mup as a character inspired by untamed childhood imagination and spirit.
