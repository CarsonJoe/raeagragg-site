About Me

Author and illustrator with a background in animation, screenwriting, and school visits for young readers.

Hello, I’m Raea! I’m a multimedia fine artist who loves nothing more than sharing my nature- inspired creations. No matter the piece or project I always emphasis the power of storytelling in my work. I’d love to come visit your school or library to discuss all things books, chat about an art commission, or just nerd out over all things creative.
